London Fashion Week

The big event for Helen in London Fashion Week was attending Victoria Beckham's show, which she really enjoyed. Here she is with Victoria back stage afterwards.

Fast & Furious Presents: Hobs & Shaw Premier

Karl Lagerfield Tribute

Helen was honoured to perform at the Karl Lagerfield Tribute staged at the iconic Grand Palais in Paris. She read from his book accomplished by the wonderful violinist Charlie Siem.

Cannes Film Festival 2019

Helen walks the red carpet at the cannes film festival with her hair coloured to match her dress.

Help Refugees

At the Cannes Film Festival Helen attended a charity event hosted by HFPA and was honoured to present a cheque to Help Refugees on their behalf.

Helen attends the BFI/Radio Times Festival

Helen attended the BFI/Radio Times Television where she was interviewed by Kristy Laing. Helen was also inducted into Radio Times Hall of Fame.
